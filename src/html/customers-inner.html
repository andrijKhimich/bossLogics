David Webb

Since 1948, David Webb has carried forward a rich tradition of design, craftsmanship and creativity as an iconic American jewelry house.

Accordingly, David Webb clients have included many of the iconic figures of our time, from Elizabeth Taylor, Jacqueline Kennedy Onassis and Diana Vreeland to Barbra Streisand and Beyoncé.

The 2010 rebirth of the David Webb brand prompted the need for modern digital solutions to showcase their high-end luxury jewelry collection.

Why Boss

A history of balancing style and function

Originally, David Webb approached us about designing and developing a digital catalog that allows consumers to view the entire collection through iPads and other tablets.

Working with David Webb's creative team every step of the way, we designed and built a digital catalog that blended lifestyle imagery with the entire David Webb product line, incorporating campaign images throughout the catalog.

Everyone at David Webb has access to the digital catalog; it’s used most frequently by sales associates in the New York and Beverly Hills stores and in the corporate office for research.

A time-sensitive challenge

More recently, when the vendor that provided David Webb’s website hosting, content management system and e-commerce functionality announced that they were closing—with just a few months before a total website shutdown—the jeweler turned to us for help.
“We wanted to work with BOSS Logics because of their existing work on our digital directory.We didn’t want to go dark, so we decided to do the simplest thing and just recreate the website. BOSS Logics went above and beyond and redesigned the site as well as made it mobile responsive. And they managed to do it all in just 2.5 months.”

Dean LeNeve

Director of Operations, David Webb

The Solution

Because David Webb’s digital catalog was already powered by the BOSS Logics platform, it made sense to rebuild their website on our platform too, introducing full e-commerce functionality along with our content management system, so David Webb could access and manage all of their content through one database.

We redesigned the David Webb site from top to bottom, restructuring the site navigation to conform to industry best practices while also redesigning the graphical treatments, page layouts, and fonts. Along the way, we changed the site from being desktop only to being mobile responsive. On the backend, we created integrations with merchant services as well as with David Webb’s existing ERP system, which does a data dump every night to an FTP site that feeds both the digital catalog and the website.

“Six weeks into the project, BOSS Logics turned over a link to a staging site. We were able to start doing merchandising & proof-reading on the site, and our teams worked together in tandem to fine tune as we got closer to launch.”

Dean LeNeve

Director of Operations, David Webb

The Launch

The new website, completely redesigned and rebuilt on the BOSS Logics platform, launched on schedule in conjunction with a series of new campaigns and creative.
“Our actual go-live was perfect. It was seamless. The integration has been amazing. Our old system involved manual data entry for everything, but with the BOSS Logics integration, it’s as easy as changing a flag and getting updates from our ERP system.”

Dean LeNeve

Director of Operations, David Webb
