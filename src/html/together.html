Introducing together for vendors

An interactive tool designed to make selling remotely feel like an in-person meeting for vendors and their retail partners.

Now more than ever, personal interactions are critical in order to maintain the relationships you’ve built. We know how important it is to create a shared experience between you and your retail partners so that you can continue to stay connected during physically distant times.

togetherTM is an interactive and virtual product sharing tool, powered by BOSS Logics, that reimagines the way vendors sell remotely through its sophisticated and interactive approach to online selling. togetherTM goes beyond video conferencing, screen sharing, and other piecemeal solutions to more closely replicate an in-person sales meeting. With together`sTM unique solution for streaming video and sharing high-resolution images, vendors can collaborate face-to-face with their retailers and share multiple products on one screen, replicating the feel of an in-person meeting.

Connect

Invite your customers to join you for private meeting appointments.

We enable one-way or two-way video to meet face-to-face throughout the experience.

You know your customers best, so to help you collaborate, the BOSS Clienteling products are integrated throughout the platform, allowing valuable and timely insight into past purchases and events such as birthdays and anniversaries.

Connect with your retailers

Organize teams and groups of buyers in our state of the art appointment system which offers full calendar and scheduling functionality to invite your retail partners to join you for private meetings.

We enable one-way or two-way video to meet face-to-face throughout the experience as you share products and stream media on one screen, combining multiple technologies into one cohesive platform.

Enter together’s reception area to experience the online equivalent of an in-person sales appointment. You’ll be able to see who’s online and have the ability to book and schedule appointments with retailers right away.

Invite & Schedule

Target your clients by using data mining and filtering to find clients matching your criteria, such as purchase history, order value, purchase date, product lines, etc.

Once you have a list of targets, send them invitations to schedule a together session. Using the together appointment system, clients select a time that works best for them.

Sell your products

Display your products in a virtual tray that will showcase all of your products. Walk your retailers through a sleek, branded virtual showroom, sharing an interactive experience where you both can highlight products, view high-resolution images, and stream media.

You can choose to either let the retailer navigate around the room or control the experience and guide the retailer as they view products alongside you, mirroring an in-person meeting. As you meet with clients, their Purchase History, Past Interactions, and Affinities will be available on your screen for your reference.

As retailers find items of interest, they are able to add them to their virtual tray and keep them present at the top of the screen just as you would be able to see your selections in person. Create a proposal at the end of your session or set up a purchase order to share with your client.

Present

Showcase products from all of your brands in the sleek interactive catalogs that BOSS is known for.

You and your team can collaboratively create Virtual Assortments for collecting products of interest before choosing the final items best suited to your customer.

Walk your customer through a branded virtual showroom, sharing an interactive experience where you both can highlight products, view high-resolution images, and stream media. You can choose to either let the customer navigate around the room and assortments or control the experience and guide the customer as the customer views alongside. And if they need a second opinion, just add a friend or family member to your together session.

As you find items of interest, add them to your virtual assortment and keep them present through the experience just as you would in-store. Purchasing is also easy: Tie into your existing POS system or simply use together's integrated heckout functionality.
